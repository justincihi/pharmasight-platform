**Tone:** The narration should be professional but passionate. Think of documentaries like "Inside Bill's Brain" or "The Social Dilemma" – serious topics delivered with clarity and conviction.

**Pacing:** The script is designed to build momentum. Start slow and somber, then accelerate as the solution is introduced, and end on a high note with the call to action.

**Visuals:** Use a mix of real footage (labs, patients, researchers) and high-quality animations (molecules, data visualizations, the PharmaSight™ interface). The visual style should be modern and clean, with a color palette of blues, whites, and subtle gradients.

**Music:** Start with a somber, contemplative score. Transition to an uplifting, inspiring score as PharmaSight™ is introduced. End with a triumphant, hopeful theme.
